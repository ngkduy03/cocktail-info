      This is a simple website as a project for Data structures and algorithms that list all informations and recipes of classic cocktails. A cocktail is an alcoholic mixed drink. Cocktails are either a combination of spirits, or one or more spirits mixed with other ingredients such as fruit juice, flavored syrup, or cream. Cocktails vary widely across regions of the world.
